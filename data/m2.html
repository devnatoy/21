Madridejos Community College (MCC) - History

Madridejos Community College (MCC), located in Madridejos, Cebu, was established with the goal of providing affordable and accessible higher education to students in the northern part of Cebu. Founded by the local government to uplift the educational standards of the municipality, MCC has offered various degree and diploma programs in education, technology, business, and general studies.

About

Madridejos School Locator and Viewer is a digital application designed to help users easily locate and view all elementary, high school, and college institutions within Madridejos. The app features an interactive map that displays the precise locations of each school, allowing users to click on a marker to view the name, photo, and basic information about the institution. It serves as a helpful tool for students, parents, and visitors who want to explore educational options in the area. This version is for viewing only, focusing on providing accurate school locations and visual references to guide users. The interface is simple, user-friendly, and ideal for both locals and newcomers.

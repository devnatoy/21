Baigad Elementary School

Baigad Elementary School is a public elementary school serving the children of Barangay Baigad. It provides quality basic education from Grades 1 to 6, ensuring that young learners receive foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and values education. The school is deeply rooted in the community and benefits from the support of dedicated teachers, parents, and local leaders. Over the years, Baigad Elementary School has played a key role in shaping the future of its students and uplifting the educational standards of the area.

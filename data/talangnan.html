Talangnan Elementary School History

Talangnan Elementary School is a public school located in Barangay Talangnan. Established many years ago, it has served as a cornerstone for education in the area, offering elementary education from Grade 1 to Grade 6. The school is known for its strong focus on academic excellence, moral development, and community involvement. During the challenging period of the COVID-19 pandemic, Talangnan Elementary School shifted to modular learning and digital platforms to ensure that education continued despite the restrictions. As the pandemic eased, the school returned to in-person classes, and since 2022, enrollment numbers have been increasing as families returned to the traditional classroom setting. The school has continued to grow and improve, fostering a supportive learning environment for the children of the community.

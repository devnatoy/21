Kodia Elementary School History

Kodia Elementary School is a public school located in a remote and rural part of the community, established to provide accessible and inclusive education to children in Grades 1 to 6. Known for its dedication to academic excellence and character formation, the school has served as a beacon of hope for many families in the barangay. The school faced considerable challenges during the COVID-19 pandemic, particularly in 2020 and 2021, but quickly adapted through modular distance learning. As conditions improved, Kodia Elementary School gradually transitioned back to face-to-face classes, regaining momentum in both enrollment and educational outcomes. With strong support from parents, teachers, and the local government, the school continues to thrive and grow.

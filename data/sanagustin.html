San Agustin Elementary School History

San Agustin Elementary School is a public educational institution situated in Barangay San Agustin. Established many years ago, the school has been a beacon of education, catering to the academic needs of young learners from Grade 1 to Grade 6. The school prides itself on its well-rounded educational approach, offering not only a strong academic curriculum but also programs for character development and community service. The school faced challenges due to the COVID-19 pandemic, transitioning to modular learning in 2020 and 2021 to continue providing quality education to its students. In 2022, as the pandemic restrictions were lifted, San Agustin Elementary School resumed face-to-face classes, which led to an increase in enrollment. The school's ability to adapt and provide consistent education has made it a reliable institution for families in the community.

San Agustin National High School - History

San Agustin National High School (SANHS), located in San Agustin, Cebu, was established with the goal of providing quality and accessible education to students in the region. It serves as a key institution for secondary education, helping to prepare students for higher learning or entry into the workforce. Over the years, SANHS has gained a reputation for its strong academic programs and extracurricular activities. The school aims to develop well-rounded students who are not only academically competent but also socially responsible and equipped with the skills needed in today's world. In response to the challenges brought by the pandemic, SANHS quickly adapted by offering online and modular learning systems, ensuring the continuity of education. As face-to-face classes resumed, the school focused on enhancing its facilities and expanding its academic offerings to meet the needs of its growing student body. SANHS continues to expand its student enrollment and improve its educational services, helping to build a brighter future for the youth of San Agustin and beyond.

Bantayan Education and Skills Training Center Inc. History

Bantayan Education and Skills Training Center Inc. has played a crucial role in providing essential skills training to the residents of Bantayan Island. Established to meet the growing demand for specialized skills in the region, the center offers various training programs, including welding, technical and vocational courses, and other livelihood skills. Over the years, it has become an indispensable resource for individuals seeking to improve their skills and secure better employment opportunities. The center's contribution to the island's development has been immense, equipping its residents with the tools they need to thrive in an ever-changing job market and contributing to the economic growth of Bantayan Island.

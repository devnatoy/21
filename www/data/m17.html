SALAZAR COLLEGES OF SCIENCE AND INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY - History

Salazar Colleges of Science and Institute of Technology (SCSIT), located in the town of Madridejos, Cebu, was established with the goal of providing quality higher education to students in the region. It was founded in the early 1990s, with the vision of offering accessible education in various fields, particularly in the fields of science and technology, to equip students with skills needed to thrive in modern industries. The institution started as a small local college but has grown significantly over the years. SCSIT is known for offering programs that are aligned with the demands of the workforce, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared for various professional roles in the fields of education, business, information technology, criminology, and other related industries.
